## Campaign 1: Sales Readiness Assessment

### Concept
**"Are You Losing Deals You Should Be Winning?"**

A 12-question assessment that reveals gaps in a salesperson's skills and shows them exactly where to focus their practice.

### Target Audience
- Individual sales reps (B2B, SaaS, enterprise)
- Sales managers evaluating their team
- SDRs/BDRs wanting to move to AE roles
- Career changers entering sales

### Landing Page Hook

**Headline:** "The 3-Minute Assessment That Reveals Why You're Losing Deals"

**Subhead:** "Join 10,000+ sales professionals who discovered their hidden skill gaps — and fixed them."

**Body:**
> Most salespeople lose 3-5 winnable deals per month without knowing why. This free assessment analyzes your skills across 5 critical areas and shows you exactly where to focus.
>
> In 3 minutes, you'll discover:
> - Your biggest skill gap costing you deals
> - Which objections you're mishandling
> - A personalized practice plan to improve

**Social Proof:** "Sales reps who identified and fixed their #1 weakness saw a 28% increase in close rates within 90 days."

**CTA:** "Take the Free Assessment →"

---

### Questions (12 total)

#### Category 1: Discovery & Qualification (3 questions)

1. **When starting a sales call, I consistently uncover the prospect's true pain points within the first 5 minutes.**
   - Yes / Sometimes / Rarely

2. **I can identify a prospect's decision-making process and timeline before presenting my solution.**
   - Yes / Sometimes / Rarely

3. **I know exactly which questions to ask to determine if a prospect is truly qualified.**
   - Yes / Sometimes / Rarely

#### Category 2: Objection Handling (3 questions)

4. **When a prospect says "it's too expensive," I have a confident, practiced response that works most of the time.**
   - Yes / Sometimes / Rarely

5. **I can handle the "I need to think about it" objection without losing momentum.**
   - Yes / Sometimes / Rarely

6. **When facing unexpected objections, I stay calm and curious rather than defensive.**
   - Yes / Sometimes / Rarely

#### Category 3: Closing & Negotiation (2 questions)

7. **I consistently ask for the close at the right moment without feeling pushy.**
   - Yes / Sometimes / Rarely

8. **When a prospect tries to negotiate price, I can hold my ground while keeping the relationship positive.**
   - Yes / Sometimes / Rarely

#### Category 4: Executive Presence (2 questions)

9. **I feel confident presenting to C-suite executives and senior decision-makers.**
   - Yes / Sometimes / Rarely

10. **I can adapt my communication style based on the seniority and personality of the person I'm speaking with.**
    - Yes / Sometimes / Rarely

#### Category 5: Emotional Intelligence (2 questions)

11. **I can tell when a prospect is losing interest or getting frustrated, and I adjust my approach accordingly.**
    - Yes / Sometimes / Rarely

12. **After a lost deal, I can objectively analyze what went wrong without taking it personally.**
    - Yes / Sometimes / Rarely

---

### Scoring Tiers

| Score | Tier | Message |
|-------|------|---------|
| 10-12 | **Sales Pro** | You've got strong fundamentals. Focus on mastering edge cases and coaching others. |
| 7-9 | **Solid Performer** | You're above average but leaving money on the table. 2-3 skill upgrades could transform your results. |
| 4-6 | **Developing** | You have good instincts but inconsistent execution. Structured practice will accelerate your growth. |
| 0-3 | **Foundation Builder** | You're early in your journey. The good news: targeted practice can rapidly close these gaps. |

## Campaign 3: Leadership Communication Assessment

### Concept
**"Are You a Leader People Want to Follow?"**

A 15-question assessment for managers and executives to evaluate their leadership communication skills across critical conversations.

### Target Audience
- New managers (promoted from IC roles)
- Middle managers
- Directors and VPs
- Executive coaches (to use with clients)
- HR / L&D professionals
- Aspiring leaders

### Landing Page Hook

**Headline:** "The Leadership Skill That Predicts Team Performance (And How to Measure Yours)"

**Subhead:** "Communication is the #1 leadership skill. This 4-minute assessment reveals your strengths and blind spots."

**Body:**
> 70% of employees say their motivation is tied to their relationship with their manager. Great leaders aren't born — they develop specific communication skills that inspire trust, drive performance, and retain top talent.
>
> This assessment evaluates you across 5 critical leadership conversation types:
> - Giving difficult feedback
> - Coaching for growth
> - Leading through change
> - Building executive presence
> - Having hard conversations (terminations, performance issues)
>
> Get your personalized Leadership Communication Score and a development roadmap.

**CTA:** "Get Your Leadership Score →"

---

## Implementation Checklist

### Phase 1: Build (Week 1)
- [ ] Set up ScoreApp account
- [ ] Create Sales Assessment first (highest volume audience)
- [ ] Design landing page with emotional hooks + stats from Roleplay Studio data
- [ ] Connect to email automation (ConvertKit, Mailchimp, etc.)
- [ ] Set up results → Roleplay Studio trial flow

### Phase 2: Test (Week 2)
- [ ] Send to 20-30 people in network for feedback
- [ ] Refine questions based on completion rate
- [ ] Test email sequence deliverability
- [ ] Verify scenario mapping makes sense

### Phase 3: Launch (Week 3+)
- [ ] LinkedIn ads targeting sales professionals
- [ ] LinkedIn organic posts about assessment
- [ ] Partner with sales trainers / coaches to share
- [ ] Add assessment link to email signatures
- [ ] Create Support and Leadership assessments

### Phase 4: Optimize (Ongoing)
- [ ] Track conversion: Assessment → Trial → Paid
- [ ] A/B test landing page headlines
- [ ] Refine email sequence based on open/click rates
- [ ] Add retargeting for non-completers
